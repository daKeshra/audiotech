About Us

instrument rentals

For over 5 years, Audiotech production, has provided Stage rentals services to amateur and professional theatrical organizations, television stations, production studios, production businesses, halls, theatres, and schools in Nigeria. We are primarily a Nigerian engineer/consultant/musical instrument hiring firm, but we also design, install, and operate stage lighting for events. If you'd like us to light your show, give us a call to talk about the details. We currently have a significant number of engineers on hand, and we are routinely hired to provide our Sound services at Crusades, concerts, weddings, burials and so on. Our rental inventory in Nigeria is growing faster than it has ever been and we will love to tune your event with our standard and magical Instrument/sound. We look forward to doing business with you.
